What to know before you switch

You get a real DigitalOcean Droplet on DigitalOcean's network and data centres - but you manage it through the bithost dashboard and the bh CLI/API, not DigitalOcean's native console. Deploy, resize, snapshot, rebuild, and power controls all live in bithost.

Support and account questions go through bithost, and your balance is prepaid rather than post-paid. For anyone who wants DigitalOcean's infrastructure without handing over a card or ID, that is the trade - and it is the point.
