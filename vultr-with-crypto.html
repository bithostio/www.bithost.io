What to know before you switch

You get a real Vultr instance on Vultr's network and data centres - but you manage it through the bithost dashboard and the bh CLI/API, not Vultr's native control panel. Deploy, resize, snapshot, rebuild, and power controls all live in bithost.

If Bitcoin through Vultr's own BitPay checkout already works for you and you are fine with a Vultr account, that is a valid path. bithost is for people who want no account, no KYC, privacy coins like Monero, and one balance across all three providers.
