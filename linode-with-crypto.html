What to know before you switch

You get a real Linode instance on Akamai's network and data centres - but you manage it through the bithost dashboard and the bh CLI/API, not the Linode/Akamai Cloud Manager. Deploy, resize, snapshot, rebuild, and power controls all live in bithost.

Support and account questions go through bithost, and your balance is prepaid rather than post-paid. For anyone who wants Linode's infrastructure without handing over a card or ID, that is the trade - and it is the point.
