Prices

To pay for BitHost servers, you must top up at least $10 in BTC, LTC, ETH, or other supported currencies. Servers are billed on per-hour basis and don't require a minimum period of usage! If you used a server for less than a month, you only pay the hour-based usage.

We will bill your BitHost servers as long as they exist, even if they are powered off. If you no longer want a server, delete it.
