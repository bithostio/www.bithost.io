Frequently Asked Questions

Who is behind bithost.io?

Bithost.io is run by Scott Robertson. All of our hosting services are managed by Digital Ocean.

Should i use a valid email?

If possible yes. We will send you reminders if your account balance is too low when servers are due to be renewed.

Server limit?

By default, all accounts are limited to how many servers they can deploy. If you would like this limit increased, then please just submit a support ticket.

Is it secure?

Because we do not store any card details, you do not need to worry about them being leaked. Your account is also protected by 2-step verification.

Why is it more expensive than Digital Ocean?

This is to mitigate the risk of converting Bitcoin back to USD, and to help with the running costs of Bithost.

Top up?

To be able to use bithost.io, you must first add some credit to your account. To do this, you must top up your account using Bitcoin. We use coinbase.com for this process.

What if my balance is too low?

If you do not have sufficient funds in your account each hour your server is running, then your server will be automatically deleted after a period of time. We will send you reminders before we do this though (so please try and use a valid email).

Where is my server password?

You are required to supply an SSH key to be able to access your server. You will have added one to your bithost account before you created the server.

Can i withdraw my balance?

Sadly we do not allow withdrawals

How often are servers billed?

Servers are billed once an hour, and the cost will be deducted from your account balance

How are the hourly prices worked out?

The hourly prices are based on the "month" prices. These month prices are 30 days worth of usage (720 hours).

If you have any more questions, please feel free to contact us.
